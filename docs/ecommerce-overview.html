Ecommerce Overview

Ecommerce is HUGE

The growth of ecommerce is out of this world! In 2017, ecommerce was responsible for around $2.3 trillion in sales and is expected to hit $4.5 trillion in 2021 (according to a Statista report). In the US alone, ecommerce represents almost 10% of retail sales and that number is expected to grow by nearly 15% each year!

But, ecommerce is not a random whirlwind of people coming and going, buying stuff online without a trace…

Everything on the internet is tracked, from optimal times of engagement, likes and reactions, click through rates, demographics to even referral traffic! And, not surprisingly, all of that data has resulted in a huge amount of ecommerce statistics you can use to grow your business.

Let’s take a look at a compiled list of some of the most important ecommerce statistics you need to know.

Ecommerce Statistics to Consider

Importance of product search

As you can see in last decade, ecommerce has seen huge jump in user base of online shopping. As there is surge in user base of online shoppers, so is there surge in the ecommerce websites. It is believe that there are more than 2M to 3M ecommerce website exists in the world. Shopify alone claim to have hosted more than 800,000 ecommerce websites. Meganto claims to have more than 230,000 ecommerce websites on its platform.

With the increase in ecommerce websites, throat cut competition has also prevailed. Ecommerces are given deep discounts to keep the users stick to their platform which makes userbase really fragile.

If a user does not find the relevant product on the top of the result page, user quickly move to another ecommerce website. It has become really important to show the highly relevant and personalized product on the top of the result page for the query user has searched for. Hence important of having powerful search feature has never been more important.

Having relevant and personalize results is important but not easy to get it right. This problem become more grave for small and medium size ecommerces where there is lack of funds and technical expertise.

Here is Unbxd fit in

Helps small and medium ecommerce business to consume their product catalog, give the relevant and personlized results based on machine learning and user's shopping behaviour.Unbxd powers this through its APIs and Analytics
